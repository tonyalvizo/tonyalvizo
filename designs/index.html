About My Designs

This features a select few of my favorite designs. All the designs you see here were designed by me and me only. It started as a hobby and grew into a passion which I pursued to make some money off of it. Although I am a CIT student, graphic design has always been one of my biggest passions. Everything I have learned about graphic design including the software that I use to create these designs has been self taught. Either through experience or through watching tutorials on YouTube. I would say I have been designing for a good 6 years but time sure does flye when you're having fun.
